# **App Name**: AutoFlow

## Core Features:

- Customer Management: Create and manage customer profiles.
- Product Management: Create and manage product inventory.
- Quotation Generation: Generate quotations for mechanical services.
- Sales Management: Manage sales transactions, including service and parts.
- Service History: Maintain a service history, showing past work and parts sold to the customer.
- Invoice Generation: Generate a simplified invoice document for each sale.
- AI-Powered Quote Suggestions: AI Tool: Predict commonly required products for quotes based on customer and vehicle profiles, along with the services needed.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Steel gray (#455A64) to evoke the strength and reliability of machinery.
- Background color: Light gray (#ECEFF1) for a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Safety orange (#FF5722) for highlighting important actions and alerts.
- Body and headline font: 'Roboto', a modern sans-serif font that is legible and professional.
- Use clear, consistent icons throughout the application to represent various actions and categories.
- A clean, modern layout to facilitate ease of use. Content should be clearly structured.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance the user experience, such as when loading new content or completing actions.
